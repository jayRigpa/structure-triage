A practice triage instrument

Which structure is carrying the weight?

A guided reading of your current practice configuration, grounded in an eleven-structure diagnostic framework. A short screening, a few rounds of questions, a dialogue to distinguish candidates — then a mapping: your presenting structure, the practice directions the framework derives for it, and its known failure modes.

New to the framework's vocabulary? Read the terms guide first (10 min)

What this is not

It does not assess or certify awakening, stages, or attainments — and it will decline if asked. It is not therapy, and not a substitute for a teacher. If you are in acute difficulty, it will stop and point you toward qualified human support instead. Structures are patterns currently carrying weight, not types of people; the map is an instrument to be used and set down.

One session, roughly 15–25 minutes. Your responses are sent to an AI model for the reading and are not stored by this site; you can export the transcript at the end.
